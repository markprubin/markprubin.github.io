Learnings and Experiences

  • Another hiatus. Easier said than done. Lot has happened over the last few months personally, which is a reason for my absence.
  • Just finished a 3rd round interview for a software consulting company. It was my first technical interview, and it didn't go as smooth as I hoped. Very basic things that are very routine became silly errors, but I am hoping that these little things don't count against me. I definitely got into my own head in certain instances, and was overthinking some aspects of the two questions I got. Once I got to the 2nd question, I only had about 7-8 minutes. I wrote half the code, and instead of letting frustration get to me and just giving up, I talked my way through the rest for the sake of time. She understood that I knew it, but I am still hoping they see that I am willing to grow and learn.
  • I really trust my soft skills and ability to work on a team, communicate effectively, and communicate with future clients. Crossing my fingers!

Reflection

  • I need to do a better job of organizaing my schedule a bit more. I tend to go all in on either a project, or working on DS&A. I need to be diligent with setting aside a few hours for my projects, taking breaks, and then setting aside time for problem solving and algorithims. I need more control, instead of letting external things dictate my learning.
  • I need to also update my work journal consistently. I have decided to change the date to the beginning of the week, so I can update it as I go, instead of at the end. Reflecting at the end of the week is great. But I do want to use it to write things down as I go, like a journal is supposed to be used for. So from here on out, the date will be the Monday of each week.
